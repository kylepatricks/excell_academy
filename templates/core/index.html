About Our School

Excel International Academy is a premier educational institution committed to academic excellence and holistic development.

Founded in 2005, we have consistently ranked among the top schools in the region, with a proven track record of outstanding academic performance and character development.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students to excel.

Our Academic Programs

Tailored learning paths for every stage of development

Early Years (3-5)

Our play-based learning approach nurtures curiosity and builds foundational skills in a safe, stimulating environment.

  • Montessori-inspired curriculum
  • Sensory learning activities
  • Social-emotional development

Primary School (6-11)

A balanced curriculum that fosters academic excellence while encouraging creativity and critical thinking.

  • Core subject mastery
  • Project-based learning
  • Character education

Secondary School (12-18)

Rigorous academic preparation combined with leadership development and career exploration.

  • Advanced placement courses
  • College counseling
  • Entrepreneurship programs
